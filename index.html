About the King Virat Kohli❤️

☛ Virat Kohli was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He showed a keen interest in cricket from a young age and started playing competitively at a very early stage.

☛ Virat is chosen for the India Under-19 team for the first time after making an impression at the youth level for Delhi. He then had a fantastic tour of England, averaging over 100 in the one-day series.

☛ In Malaysia's Under-19 World Cup, Virat led India to win and finished the competition as one of the top three run scorers. His innate leadership abilities attracted the proper kind of attention.

☛ Royal Challengers Bangalore, an IPL team, signs Virat on a youngster contract for just Rs 12.5 lakhs when he is still a minor.

☛ Kohli made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in August 2008 in an ODI against Sri Lanka. He quickly established himself as a promising talent.

☛ When Virat got his first ODI century against Sri Lanka, 107 off 111, it was worth the wait for him to return to the national squad.

☛Virat Kohli is known for his consistency and extraordinary batting skills. He has numerous records to his name, including being the fastest to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000,12,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs).

☛Kohli has captained the Indian cricket team in all three formats (Test, ODI, and T20I). Under his leadership, India achieved significant success, including historic Test series wins in Australia.

☛ Kohli is known for his remarkable fitness levels and rigorous training routines. His commitment to fitness has inspired many aspiring cricketers to prioritize physical conditioning.

☛ Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, and the couple has a daughter named Vamika.

☛ Kohli is actively involved in social causes and has used his platform to raise awareness about various issues, including fitness and mental health.

☛ Kohli has received numerous awards, including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times. He has also been named in the ICC World XI in various formats.

☛ In Test cricket, Virat Kohli has been a prolific run-scorer and has consistently been ranked among the best batsmen in the world. He has scored a significant number of centuries in the format.

☛ Kohli is renowned for his ability to chase down targets in limited-overs cricket. He has a remarkable success rate when batting second in ODIs and T20Is.
